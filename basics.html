Before diving into the specifics of the grids, it is important to understand what breakpoints are. These are especially important when you are building your web to be mobile responsive. These breakpoints are extremely important to pay attention to as they are the points in which your layout would change. There is a business case for this! Take your time to resize the window and look at the inspect panel to view the different types of grids.
